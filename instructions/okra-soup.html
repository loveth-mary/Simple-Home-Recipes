Okra Soup

Home Recipes Meal Plan Submit Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fresh okra (chopped or blended)
  • Palm oil
  • Onions
  • Crayfish
  • Stockfish
  • Dry fish
  • Beef or assorted meat
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Water or stock
  • Scent leaves or ugu (optional)

Preparation

  1. Wash and slice okra into small pieces.
  2. Prepare your proteins:
    • Meat
    • Stockfish
    • Dry fish
  3. Season and cook meat untill tender. keep the stock.
  4. Blend pepper and onions.

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat palm oil on a pot.
  2. Add blended pepper and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add cooked meat, Stockfish, and dry fish.
  4. Pour in meat stock and allow to boil.
  5. Add:
    • Crayfish
    • Seasoning cubes
    • Salt
  6. Add the sliced okra.
  7. Stir and cook for a few minutes.
  8. Add ugu or spinach (optional).
  9. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
    10. Soup is ready when it becomes slightly thick and drawy.
