Tomato Stew

Home Recipes Meal Plan Submit Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tomato paste (optional for richer color)
  • Chicken, beef, or fish
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Curry powder
  • Water or stock

Preparation

  1. Wash and blend:
    • Fresh tomatoes
    • Red bell peppers
    • Scotch bonnet peppers
    • Onions
  2. Boil the blended mixtures untill the water reduces and it becomes thick, Set aside.
  3. Wash and season your meat (beef/chicken/fish) with:
    • salt
    • Seasoning cubes
    • Onions
    Cook untill tender and keep the stock.
  4. Slice extra onions for frying.

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pot.
  2. Add sliced onions and fry until soft.
  3. Add the boiled tomato mix and fry well.
  4. Fry until:
    • Water dries up
    • Oil begins to separate (very important)
  5. Add:
    • Seasoning cubes
    • Salt
    • Curry
    • Thyme
  6. Add cooked meat or fish.
  7. Add a little meat stock to adjust consistency.
  8. Stir and allow to simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    9. Stew is ready when oil floats on top and taste is rich.
