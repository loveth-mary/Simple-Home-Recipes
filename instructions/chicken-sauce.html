Chicken Sauce

Home Recipes Meal Plan Submit Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet peppers (optional for heat)
  • Carrots
  • Green Peas
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Water or chicken stock

Preparation

  1. Wash and season chicken.
  2. Cook or fry chicken untill golden brown.
  3. Chop vegetables:
    • Carrots
    • Green beans
    • Bell peppers
    • Onions

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil.
  2. Add onions.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add pepper mix.
  5. Add:
    • Curry
    • Thyme
    • Seasoning cubes
    • Salt
  6. Add little water or stock.
  7. Add chicken.
  8. Mix everything well.
  9. Allow to simmer for 5-10 minutes.
    10. Sauce should be slightly tick not watery.
