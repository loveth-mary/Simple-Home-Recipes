Catfish Pepper Soup

Home Recipes Meal Plan Submit Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fresh catfish, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet peppers or chili peppers
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Uda seeds (optional)
  • Ehuru seeds (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Locust beans (iru) - optional for flavor
  • Fresh scent leaves or basil
  • Lemon or lime juice (optional, for tanginess)
  • Water

Preparation

  1. Wash catfish with warm water and salt/lemon (to remove lime).
  2. Cut into pieces.
  3. Blend pepper, onions, and a little garlic.
  4. Get pepper soup spice resdy.

Cooking Steps

  1. Place catfishin a pot.
  2. Add:
    • Blended pepper mix
    • Pepper soup spice
    • Seasoning cubes
    • Salt
  3. Add water (just enough to cover fish).
  4. Allow to boil gently (do not over-stir, fish is soft).
  5. Add scent leaves or uziza leaves.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  7. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    8. Ready when fish is tender and broth is rich and spicy.
