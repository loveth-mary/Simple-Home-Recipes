Fried Rice

Home Recipes Meal Plan Submit Recipe

Ingrediants

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Green Peas
  • Onions
  • Sweet corn
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or shrimps
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Water

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and parboil it untill it is about 70% cooked. Drain and rinse with clean water . set aside.
  2. Seasen and cook the chicken or shrimps with salt, Seasoning cubes, curry, and thyme. Once done, cut into small pieces.
  3. Chop all vegetables (Carrots, green peas, onions, peppers, and sweet corn) into small pieces.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add chopped onions and peppers and fry for a few seconds untill fragrant.
  6. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked chicken or shrimps and mix well.
  8. Add curry powder,thyme, Seasoning cubes, and salt. Stir everything togrther inside the oil.
  9. Add the parboiled rice and begin to fry by stirring continously so it doesn't burn.
  10. Keep stirring and turning the rice so it fries evenly and mixes well with the ingredients.
  11. Add a little water or stock if needed, do not make it too soft.
  12. Continue frying and stirring untill the rice is fully cooked, dry and well combined.
  13. Remove from heat and serve hot.
